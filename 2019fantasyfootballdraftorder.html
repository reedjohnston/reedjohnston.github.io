2019 Fantasy Football Draft Order Selection


NEW THIS YEAR: We'll be using the Little League World Series (LLWS) to determine our draft order this year.
  • The tournament runs from August 15th-25th
  • It includes 16 teams
  • 8 teams representing the champions from the regional tournaments in the Unites States
  • 8 teams representing the International country/region champions

Team Selection Process:
  • Names will be drawn from a hat assigning you to a LLWS Team
  • After the 10 selections are complete, the remaining 6 LLWS teams will be ignored
  • Top seed for the draft will go to the person assigned to the LLWS team that finishes with the highest placing in the tournament.


LLWS Rankings determined as follows:

First: Number of Total Wins
Second: Runs Scored
Third: Runs Against
Fourth: Finally, if Trump would deport your team, then you get the higher seed
