2019 Fantasy Football Draft Order Selection


We'll be using the Little League World Series (LLWS) to determine our draft order this year.

The tournament runs from August 15th-25th. It includes 16 teams; 8 teams representing the champions from the regional tournaments in the Unites States and 8 teams representing the International country/region champions.

We'll be drawing our names from a hat and assigning them to LLWS teams drawn from another hat. After all ten names are drawn and assigned to a LLWS team we will simply ignore the remaining 6 LLWS teams in the hat. Top seed for the draft will go to the person assigned to the LLWS team that finishes with the highest placing in the tournament.

Tie breakers are as follows:

First: # of Wins
Second: Runs Scored
Third: Runs Against
Fourth: If Trump would deport your team then you get the higher seed
